About

About me, what I have achieved and why I created this website.

Why Finance

When I came to DePaul University, I was only certain of one thing, that I would pursue a major within the school of business. I started with Accounting and I went on to declare every other major at one point in time during my first two years at DePaul. However, my final stop was Finance. I chose it because I thought Finance was an overall solid major that would allow me to exit into anything. It was only at the end of my sophomore year that I realized why I truly chose Finance. It was because of the stimulation, the constant changing markets and securities landscape. The technological transformations and the adapting rules and everything in between captivated me. There was so much to learn, and I have never been as eager to learn as I am now.

Why This Website

I created this website as a place to share my knowledge and experiences. I do not have social media as I am not a fan of it, so I created my own. I also see this as an opportunity to improve my HTML skills as I design and integrate new code to support my objectives. My goal will be to create research papers, informative articles and opinions pieces and use this website as a place of storage for everything that I create. I will not, however, promote or market this website.
