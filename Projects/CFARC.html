"DePaul University Team Wins Third Place at the CFA Society Chicago Research Challenge"

Our team gave our virtual Zoom pitch at 10am on Friday February 26th to a group of judges. We were given 10 minutes to present and 10 minutes for Q&A. We practiced our pitch a dozen of times that week and by 10am we were fired up and ready to go. Our presentation ended perfectly as time expired and we went straight into the Q&A. We already anticipated certain questions so we answered those questions with ease. By the end of the Q&A it was clear to everyone that we were on top of our research and our hard work was shown off. At the result of the Zoom pitch we were all pumped up and knew that no matter the result we made a name for ourselves. Later that day, we joined the Awards Ceremony where it was announced that our team won 3rd place in the competition. For our efforts we received a 3rd place plaque, which will go to DePaul's Finance Department, and a $25 gift card.

Overall, the CFARC was an unforgettable expereince. I learned so much, made great friendships with my teammates, and learned about the career that I now want to pursue. A huge thanks is due to James Valentine, our mentor and faculty advisor, without him none of this would have been possible. It was an honor to represent DePaul and etch our names into DePaul's history books as the first DePaul team to place Top 3 at the CFARC.
