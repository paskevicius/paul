(PII) 12-14-2020: Upper Peninsula Trip Confirms Supply Chain Issues

On December 11th, my roommates and I took a short weekend vacation to travel around Lake Michigan. One of our destinations was the Upper Peninsula (UP). We were planning on ice fishing so we stopped at a local fishing gear store. They were also a distributor of various products including ORV's, snowmobiles, augers, etc. While discussing with the show owner I asked him about their line of Polaris Ranger XP's and Polaris snowmobiles since they are popular up north. The owner told me that they are completely out of stock and are backlogged on backorders and have not had any shipments for over a month. This confirms my earlier findings that COVID has distrupted the supply chain between Suppliers and Distributors for Polaris. However, on a positive note, the owner told me that the demand for snowmobiles and ORV's has atleast doubled compared to last year. The demand is there but the supply is far behind.

I would also like to urge caution that this was only one store that I visited, so it could be an outlier. However, prior research has indicated the same findings so take this information as is.

Opinion: To me this indicates that the demand for outdoor recreational vehicles is only increasing but at a pace that can not match the supply. For PII, the supply chain is a material critical factor that I will have to keep an eye on but I am maintaining my BUY recommendation.
